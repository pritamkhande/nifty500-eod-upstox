System Behaviour Commentary

For COCHINSHIP, the system generated 30 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.5 bars. With a win rate of 53.3% and an average outcome of 0.96R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
