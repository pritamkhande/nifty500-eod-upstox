System Behaviour Commentary

For NBCC, the system generated 39 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 24.6 bars. With a win rate of 43.6% and an average outcome of 0.17R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -8.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
