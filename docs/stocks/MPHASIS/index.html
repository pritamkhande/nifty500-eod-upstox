System Behaviour Commentary

For MPHASIS, the system generated 62 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 39.4 bars. With a win rate of 54.8% and an average outcome of 0.77R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
