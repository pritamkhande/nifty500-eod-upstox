System Behaviour Commentary

For POLYMED, the system generated 41 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.1 bars. With a win rate of 53.7% and an average outcome of 0.64R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
