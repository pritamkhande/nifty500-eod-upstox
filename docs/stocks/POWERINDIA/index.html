System Behaviour Commentary

For POWERINDIA, the system generated 12 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.4 bars. With a win rate of 58.3% and an average outcome of 1.18R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
