System Behaviour Commentary

For ATGL, the system generated 23 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.5 bars. With a win rate of 91.3% and an average outcome of 1.50R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 9.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -0.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
