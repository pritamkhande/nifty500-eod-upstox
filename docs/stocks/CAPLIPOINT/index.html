System Behaviour Commentary

For CAPLIPOINT, the system generated 33 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 26.7 bars. With a win rate of 57.6% and an average outcome of 0.52R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
