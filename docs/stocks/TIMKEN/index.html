System Behaviour Commentary

For TIMKEN, the system generated 53 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.7 bars. With a win rate of 66.0% and an average outcome of 0.82R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
