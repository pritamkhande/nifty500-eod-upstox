System Behaviour Commentary

For POLICYBZR, the system generated 11 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 37.4 bars. With a win rate of 81.8% and an average outcome of 1.04R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
