System Behaviour Commentary

For SWANCORP, the system generated 35 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.3 bars. With a win rate of 60.0% and an average outcome of 0.39R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
