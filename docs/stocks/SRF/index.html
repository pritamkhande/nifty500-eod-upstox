System Behaviour Commentary

For SRF, the system generated 60 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 37.4 bars. With a win rate of 71.7% and an average outcome of 1.01R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
