System Behaviour Commentary

For PGHH, the system generated 44 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 51.5 bars. With a win rate of 56.8% and an average outcome of 0.71R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -7.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
