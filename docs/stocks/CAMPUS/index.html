System Behaviour Commentary

For CAMPUS, the system generated 13 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 36.8 bars. With a win rate of 76.9% and an average outcome of 1.24R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 8.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
