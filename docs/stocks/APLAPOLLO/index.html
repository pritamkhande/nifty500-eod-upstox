System Behaviour Commentary

For APLAPOLLO, the system generated 29 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 39.6 bars. With a win rate of 65.5% and an average outcome of 1.01R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
