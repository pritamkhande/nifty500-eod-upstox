System Behaviour Commentary

For NH, the system generated 25 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 47.5 bars. With a win rate of 64.0% and an average outcome of 0.69R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
