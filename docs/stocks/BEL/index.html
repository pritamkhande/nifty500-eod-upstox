System Behaviour Commentary

For BEL, the system generated 35 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 27.1 bars. With a win rate of 51.4% and an average outcome of 0.20R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.6%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
