System Behaviour Commentary

For MCX, the system generated 32 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 25.5 bars. With a win rate of 53.1% and an average outcome of 0.56R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
