System Behaviour Commentary

For GODIGIT, the system generated 4 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 45.8 bars. With a win rate of 25.0% and an average outcome of -0.07R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly -0.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
