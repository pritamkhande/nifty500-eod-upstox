System Behaviour Commentary

For ERIS, the system generated 22 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 39.9 bars. With a win rate of 45.5% and an average outcome of 0.69R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
