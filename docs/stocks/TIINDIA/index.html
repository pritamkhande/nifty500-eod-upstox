System Behaviour Commentary

For TIINDIA, the system generated 24 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.5 bars. With a win rate of 58.3% and an average outcome of 0.53R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
