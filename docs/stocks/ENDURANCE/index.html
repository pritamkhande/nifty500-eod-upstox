System Behaviour Commentary

For ENDURANCE, the system generated 20 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 43.9 bars. With a win rate of 60.0% and an average outcome of 1.18R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
