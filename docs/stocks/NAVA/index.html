System Behaviour Commentary

For NAVA, the system generated 59 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.6 bars. With a win rate of 55.9% and an average outcome of 0.52R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
