System Behaviour Commentary

For SUNPHARMA, the system generated 65 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 40.2 bars. With a win rate of 63.1% and an average outcome of 0.85R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
