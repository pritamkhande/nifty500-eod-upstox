System Behaviour Commentary

For NAUKRI, the system generated 53 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.0 bars. With a win rate of 56.6% and an average outcome of 0.36R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
