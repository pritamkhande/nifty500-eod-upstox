System Behaviour Commentary

For UBL, the system generated 45 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.9 bars. With a win rate of 64.4% and an average outcome of 0.50R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
