System Behaviour Commentary

For STARHEALTH, the system generated 13 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.4 bars. With a win rate of 84.6% and an average outcome of 1.23R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -0.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
