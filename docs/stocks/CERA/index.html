System Behaviour Commentary

For CERA, the system generated 39 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 42.3 bars. With a win rate of 48.7% and an average outcome of 0.61R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
