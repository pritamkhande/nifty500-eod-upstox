System Behaviour Commentary

For PAYTM, the system generated 10 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.1 bars. With a win rate of 60.0% and an average outcome of 1.98R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 8.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
