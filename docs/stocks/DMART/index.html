System Behaviour Commentary

For DMART, the system generated 22 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 25.7 bars. With a win rate of 72.7% and an average outcome of 0.98R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
