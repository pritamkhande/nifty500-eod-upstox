System Behaviour Commentary

For NMDC, the system generated 29 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.1 bars. With a win rate of 65.5% and an average outcome of 0.47R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
