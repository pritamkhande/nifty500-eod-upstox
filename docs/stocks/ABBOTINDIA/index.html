System Behaviour Commentary

For ABBOTINDIA, the system generated 33 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 41.1 bars. With a win rate of 45.5% and an average outcome of 0.63R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
