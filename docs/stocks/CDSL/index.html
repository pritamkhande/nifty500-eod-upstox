System Behaviour Commentary

For CDSL, the system generated 26 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.5 bars. With a win rate of 57.7% and an average outcome of 1.18R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 7.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
