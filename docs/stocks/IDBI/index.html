System Behaviour Commentary

For IDBI, the system generated 95 trades over the full sample, averaging about 4.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.6 bars. With a win rate of 60.0% and an average outcome of 0.55R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -12.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
