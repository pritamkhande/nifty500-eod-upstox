System Behaviour Commentary

For ETERNAL, the system generated 17 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.1 bars. With a win rate of 76.5% and an average outcome of 1.19R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 9.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
