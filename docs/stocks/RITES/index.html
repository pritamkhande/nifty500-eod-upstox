System Behaviour Commentary

For RITES, the system generated 32 trades over the full sample, averaging about 4.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 28.0 bars. With a win rate of 62.5% and an average outcome of 0.11R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
