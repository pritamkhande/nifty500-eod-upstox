System Behaviour Commentary

For HEG, the system generated 55 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.1 bars. With a win rate of 56.4% and an average outcome of 0.54R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.6%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
