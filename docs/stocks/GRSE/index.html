System Behaviour Commentary

For GRSE, the system generated 19 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.8 bars. With a win rate of 73.7% and an average outcome of 0.78R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
