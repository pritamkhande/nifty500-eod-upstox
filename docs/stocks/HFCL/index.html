System Behaviour Commentary

For HFCL, the system generated 52 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 28.4 bars. With a win rate of 57.7% and an average outcome of 0.35R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
