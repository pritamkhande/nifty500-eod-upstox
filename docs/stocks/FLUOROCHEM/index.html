System Behaviour Commentary

For FLUOROCHEM, the system generated 12 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.8 bars. With a win rate of 58.3% and an average outcome of 1.14R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
