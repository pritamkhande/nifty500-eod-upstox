System Behaviour Commentary

For INDUSTOWER, the system generated 33 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 45.7 bars. With a win rate of 75.8% and an average outcome of 1.01R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
