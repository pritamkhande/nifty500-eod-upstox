System Behaviour Commentary

For SBFC, the system generated 9 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.7 bars. With a win rate of 55.6% and an average outcome of 0.24R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
