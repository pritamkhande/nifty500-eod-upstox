System Behaviour Commentary

For 360ONE, the system generated 17 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.5 bars. With a win rate of 70.6% and an average outcome of 0.53R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
