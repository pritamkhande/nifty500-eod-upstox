System Behaviour Commentary

For GLENMARK, the system generated 63 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 39.1 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
