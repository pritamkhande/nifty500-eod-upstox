System Behaviour Commentary

For TARIL, the system generated 15 trades over the full sample, averaging about 0.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.0 bars. With a win rate of 40.0% and an average outcome of 0.66R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
