System Behaviour Commentary

For AWL, the system generated 13 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 26.9 bars. With a win rate of 61.5% and an average outcome of 0.63R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
