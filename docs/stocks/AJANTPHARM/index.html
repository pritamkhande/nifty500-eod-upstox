System Behaviour Commentary

For AJANTPHARM, the system generated 34 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.5 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
