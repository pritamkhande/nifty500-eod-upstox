System Behaviour Commentary

For BERGEPAINT, the system generated 43 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 46.7 bars. With a win rate of 69.8% and an average outcome of 0.99R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
