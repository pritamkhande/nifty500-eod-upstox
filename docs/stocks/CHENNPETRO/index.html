System Behaviour Commentary

For CHENNPETRO, the system generated 75 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.7 bars. With a win rate of 69.3% and an average outcome of 1.15R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 7.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
