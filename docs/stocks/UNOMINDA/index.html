System Behaviour Commentary

For UNOMINDA, the system generated 30 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 44.1 bars. With a win rate of 70.0% and an average outcome of 1.11R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
