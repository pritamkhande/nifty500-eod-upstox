System Behaviour Commentary

For SBILIFE, the system generated 18 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.7 bars. With a win rate of 55.6% and an average outcome of 0.45R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
