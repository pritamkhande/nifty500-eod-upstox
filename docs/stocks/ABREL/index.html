System Behaviour Commentary

For ABREL, the system generated 61 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.3 bars. With a win rate of 63.9% and an average outcome of 1.21R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -8.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
