System Behaviour Commentary

For ACE, the system generated 64 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.5 bars. With a win rate of 57.8% and an average outcome of 0.81R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
