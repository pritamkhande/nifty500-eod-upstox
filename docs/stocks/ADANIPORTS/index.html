System Behaviour Commentary

For ADANIPORTS, the system generated 65 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.4 bars. With a win rate of 69.2% and an average outcome of 0.79R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
