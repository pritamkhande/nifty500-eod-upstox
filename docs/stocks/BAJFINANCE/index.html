System Behaviour Commentary

For BAJFINANCE, the system generated 26 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.1 bars. With a win rate of 69.2% and an average outcome of 1.33R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
