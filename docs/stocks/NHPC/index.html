System Behaviour Commentary

For NHPC, the system generated 19 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.3 bars. With a win rate of 68.4% and an average outcome of 0.28R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
