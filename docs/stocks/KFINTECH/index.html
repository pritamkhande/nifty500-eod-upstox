System Behaviour Commentary

For KFINTECH, the system generated 9 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 43.1 bars. With a win rate of 77.8% and an average outcome of 1.39R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
