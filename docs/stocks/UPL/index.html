System Behaviour Commentary

For UPL, the system generated 81 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.5 bars. With a win rate of 58.0% and an average outcome of 0.66R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -9.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
