System Behaviour Commentary

For ASTRAL, the system generated 29 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 47.2 bars. With a win rate of 72.4% and an average outcome of 1.59R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
