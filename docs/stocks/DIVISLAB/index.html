System Behaviour Commentary

For DIVISLAB, the system generated 64 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.7 bars. With a win rate of 64.1% and an average outcome of 0.60R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
