System Behaviour Commentary

For HAPPSTMNDS, the system generated 14 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 25.9 bars. With a win rate of 57.1% and an average outcome of 0.74R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
