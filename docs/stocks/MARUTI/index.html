System Behaviour Commentary

For MARUTI, the system generated 55 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 28.1 bars. With a win rate of 54.5% and an average outcome of 0.75R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -12.6%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
