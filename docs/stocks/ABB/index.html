System Behaviour Commentary

For ABB, the system generated 58 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 41.8 bars. With a win rate of 70.7% and an average outcome of 1.57R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 7.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -7.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
