System Behaviour Commentary

For HDFCBANK, the system generated 76 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.7 bars. With a win rate of 61.8% and an average outcome of 0.71R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
