System Behaviour Commentary

For CUB, the system generated 54 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.3 bars. With a win rate of 44.4% and an average outcome of 0.34R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
