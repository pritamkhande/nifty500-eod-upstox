System Behaviour Commentary

For GPIL, the system generated 28 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 27.5 bars. With a win rate of 57.1% and an average outcome of 0.51R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
