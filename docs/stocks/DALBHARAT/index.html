System Behaviour Commentary

For DALBHARAT, the system generated 17 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.6 bars. With a win rate of 82.4% and an average outcome of 0.94R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
