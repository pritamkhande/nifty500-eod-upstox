System Behaviour Commentary

For APLLTD, the system generated 37 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 40.3 bars. With a win rate of 59.5% and an average outcome of 0.71R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
