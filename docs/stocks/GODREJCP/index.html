System Behaviour Commentary

For GODREJCP, the system generated 58 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.5 bars. With a win rate of 43.1% and an average outcome of 0.19R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
