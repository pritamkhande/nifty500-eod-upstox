System Behaviour Commentary

For LTTS, the system generated 23 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.1 bars. With a win rate of 73.9% and an average outcome of 1.27R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
