System Behaviour Commentary

For BLS, the system generated 33 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.9 bars. With a win rate of 66.7% and an average outcome of 0.78R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
