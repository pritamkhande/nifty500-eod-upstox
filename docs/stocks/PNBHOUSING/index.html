System Behaviour Commentary

For PNBHOUSING, the system generated 22 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.4 bars. With a win rate of 68.2% and an average outcome of 1.12R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
