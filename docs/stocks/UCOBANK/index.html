System Behaviour Commentary

For UCOBANK, the system generated 52 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.4 bars. With a win rate of 63.5% and an average outcome of 0.85R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
