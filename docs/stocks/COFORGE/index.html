System Behaviour Commentary

For COFORGE, the system generated 50 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.3 bars. With a win rate of 54.0% and an average outcome of 0.57R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
