System Behaviour Commentary

For KPITTECH, the system generated 16 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 40.1 bars. With a win rate of 75.0% and an average outcome of 1.61R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 7.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
