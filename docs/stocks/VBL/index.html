System Behaviour Commentary

For VBL, the system generated 25 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.6 bars. With a win rate of 52.0% and an average outcome of 0.40R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
