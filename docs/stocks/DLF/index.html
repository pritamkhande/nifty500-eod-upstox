System Behaviour Commentary

For DLF, the system generated 56 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.0 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.8 bars. With a win rate of 73.2% and an average outcome of 1.05R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
