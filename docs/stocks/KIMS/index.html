System Behaviour Commentary

For KIMS, the system generated 13 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.1 bars. With a win rate of 53.8% and an average outcome of 0.42R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
