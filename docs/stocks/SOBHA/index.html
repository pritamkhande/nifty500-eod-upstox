System Behaviour Commentary

For SOBHA, the system generated 55 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.3 bars. With a win rate of 69.1% and an average outcome of 0.83R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
