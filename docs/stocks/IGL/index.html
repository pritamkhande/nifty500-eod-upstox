System Behaviour Commentary

For IGL, the system generated 46 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.3 bars. With a win rate of 50.0% and an average outcome of 0.48R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
