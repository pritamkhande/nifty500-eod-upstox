System Behaviour Commentary

For NEWGEN, the system generated 20 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 45.2 bars. With a win rate of 75.0% and an average outcome of 2.05R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 10.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
