System Behaviour Commentary

For IRCON, the system generated 21 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.9 bars. With a win rate of 71.4% and an average outcome of 1.33R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 7.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
