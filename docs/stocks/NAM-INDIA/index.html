System Behaviour Commentary

For NAM-INDIA, the system generated 25 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.0 bars. With a win rate of 76.0% and an average outcome of 0.92R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
