System Behaviour Commentary

For HDFCAMC, the system generated 16 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.3 bars. With a win rate of 56.2% and an average outcome of 0.89R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
