System Behaviour Commentary

For MAZDOCK, the system generated 15 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.3 bars. With a win rate of 73.3% and an average outcome of 1.92R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
