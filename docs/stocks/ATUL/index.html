System Behaviour Commentary

For ATUL, the system generated 60 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 40.3 bars. With a win rate of 63.3% and an average outcome of 0.98R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
