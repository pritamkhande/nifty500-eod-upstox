System Behaviour Commentary

For MGL, the system generated 21 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 37.6 bars. With a win rate of 85.7% and an average outcome of 1.63R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 7.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
