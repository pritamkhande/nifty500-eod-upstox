System Behaviour Commentary

For RAILTEL, the system generated 16 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.9 bars. With a win rate of 62.5% and an average outcome of 1.04R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 7.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
