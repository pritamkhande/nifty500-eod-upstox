System Behaviour Commentary

For BDL, the system generated 29 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 28.1 bars. With a win rate of 62.1% and an average outcome of 0.62R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
