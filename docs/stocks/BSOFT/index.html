System Behaviour Commentary

For BSOFT, the system generated 65 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.2 bars. With a win rate of 56.9% and an average outcome of 0.74R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
