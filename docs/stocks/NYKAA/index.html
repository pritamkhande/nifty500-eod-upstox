System Behaviour Commentary

For NYKAA, the system generated 14 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.4 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.6 bars. With a win rate of 42.9% and an average outcome of 0.36R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
