System Behaviour Commentary

For FEDERALBNK, the system generated 64 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 27.0 bars. With a win rate of 45.3% and an average outcome of 0.17R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -10.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
