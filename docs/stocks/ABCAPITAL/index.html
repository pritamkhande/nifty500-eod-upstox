System Behaviour Commentary

For ABCAPITAL, the system generated 39 trades over the full sample, averaging about 4.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 26.2 bars. With a win rate of 69.2% and an average outcome of 0.52R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
