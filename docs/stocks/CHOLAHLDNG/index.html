System Behaviour Commentary

For CHOLAHLDNG, the system generated 18 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 43.8 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
