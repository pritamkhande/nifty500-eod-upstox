System Behaviour Commentary

For GLAND, the system generated 10 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.0 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 37.5 bars. With a win rate of 50.0% and an average outcome of 0.97R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
