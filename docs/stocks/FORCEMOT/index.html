System Behaviour Commentary

For FORCEMOT, the system generated 12 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 43.9 bars. With a win rate of 75.0% and an average outcome of 3.71R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 13.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
