System Behaviour Commentary

For ALKEM, the system generated 20 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.0 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 50.0 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
