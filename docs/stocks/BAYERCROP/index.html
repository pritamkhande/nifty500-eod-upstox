System Behaviour Commentary

For BAYERCROP, the system generated 33 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.9 bars. With a win rate of 48.5% and an average outcome of 0.60R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
