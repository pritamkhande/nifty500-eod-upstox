System Behaviour Commentary

For POLYCAB, the system generated 15 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 36.2 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
