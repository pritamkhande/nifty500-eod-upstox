System Behaviour Commentary

For MINDACORP, the system generated 43 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 36.7 bars. With a win rate of 55.8% and an average outcome of 0.42R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
