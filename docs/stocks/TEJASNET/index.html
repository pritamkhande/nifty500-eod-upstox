System Behaviour Commentary

For TEJASNET, the system generated 23 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.6 bars. With a win rate of 78.3% and an average outcome of 1.23R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
