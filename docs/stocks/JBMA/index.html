System Behaviour Commentary

For JBMA, the system generated 34 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.0 bars. With a win rate of 55.9% and an average outcome of 0.49R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.6%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
