System Behaviour Commentary

For CHALET, the system generated 16 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 41.5 bars. With a win rate of 62.5% and an average outcome of 0.90R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
