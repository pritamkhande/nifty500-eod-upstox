System Behaviour Commentary

For ARE&M, the system generated 60 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.3 bars. With a win rate of 58.3% and an average outcome of 0.63R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
