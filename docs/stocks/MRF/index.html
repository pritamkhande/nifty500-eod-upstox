System Behaviour Commentary

For MRF, the system generated 48 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 25.8 bars. With a win rate of 62.5% and an average outcome of 0.80R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
