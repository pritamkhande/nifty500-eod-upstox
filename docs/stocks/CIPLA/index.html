System Behaviour Commentary

For CIPLA, the system generated 66 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 36.6 bars. With a win rate of 69.7% and an average outcome of 0.69R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.6%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
