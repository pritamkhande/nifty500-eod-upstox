System Behaviour Commentary

For TATAINVEST, the system generated 40 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.4 bars. With a win rate of 57.5% and an average outcome of 0.42R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -6.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
