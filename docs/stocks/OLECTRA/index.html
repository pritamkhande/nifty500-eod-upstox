System Behaviour Commentary

For OLECTRA, the system generated 41 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 25.4 bars. With a win rate of 53.7% and an average outcome of 0.68R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
