System Behaviour Commentary

For BLUEDART, the system generated 50 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.4 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
