System Behaviour Commentary

For INOXWIND, the system generated 27 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.7 bars. With a win rate of 44.4% and an average outcome of 0.44R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
