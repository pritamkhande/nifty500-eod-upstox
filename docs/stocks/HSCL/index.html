System Behaviour Commentary

For HSCL, the system generated 60 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.5 bars. With a win rate of 55.0% and an average outcome of 0.46R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
