System Behaviour Commentary

For BSE, the system generated 19 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 39.1 bars. With a win rate of 68.4% and an average outcome of 3.09R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 11.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
