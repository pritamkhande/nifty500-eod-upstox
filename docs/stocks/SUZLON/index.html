System Behaviour Commentary

For SUZLON, the system generated 40 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.0 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 26.4 bars. With a win rate of 55.0% and an average outcome of 0.77R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
