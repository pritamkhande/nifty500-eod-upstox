System Behaviour Commentary

For SAREGAMA, the system generated 35 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.6 bars. With a win rate of 54.3% and an average outcome of 0.68R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.7%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
