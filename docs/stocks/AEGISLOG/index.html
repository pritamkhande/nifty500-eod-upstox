System Behaviour Commentary

For AEGISLOG, the system generated 39 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 37.4 bars. With a win rate of 71.8% and an average outcome of 0.68R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
