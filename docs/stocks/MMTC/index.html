System Behaviour Commentary

For MMTC, the system generated 49 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.1 bars. With a win rate of 63.3% and an average outcome of 0.60R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
