System Behaviour Commentary

For RBLBANK, the system generated 30 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.3 bars. With a win rate of 73.3% and an average outcome of 0.97R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
