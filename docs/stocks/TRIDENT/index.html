System Behaviour Commentary

For TRIDENT, the system generated 13 trades over the full sample, averaging about 0.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 28.9 bars. With a win rate of 76.9% and an average outcome of 0.44R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
