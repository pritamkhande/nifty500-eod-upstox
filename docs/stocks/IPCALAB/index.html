System Behaviour Commentary

For IPCALAB, the system generated 57 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.9 bars. With a win rate of 59.6% and an average outcome of 0.35R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -7.1%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
