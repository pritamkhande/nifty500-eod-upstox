System Behaviour Commentary

For PFC, the system generated 88 trades over the full sample, averaging about 4.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.3 bars. With a win rate of 61.4% and an average outcome of 0.60R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
