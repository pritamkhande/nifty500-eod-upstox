System Behaviour Commentary

For IIFL, the system generated 67 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.8 bars. With a win rate of 53.7% and an average outcome of 0.49R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
