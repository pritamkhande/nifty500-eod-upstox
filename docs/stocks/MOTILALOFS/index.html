System Behaviour Commentary

For MOTILALOFS, the system generated 45 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.2 bars. With a win rate of 55.6% and an average outcome of 0.73R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.5% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -9.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
