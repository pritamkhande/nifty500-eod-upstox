System Behaviour Commentary

For MAPMYINDIA, the system generated 9 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 25.6 bars. With a win rate of 44.4% and an average outcome of 0.21R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 0.9% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
