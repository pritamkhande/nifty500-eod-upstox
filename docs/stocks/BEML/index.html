System Behaviour Commentary

For BEML, the system generated 61 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.7 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.5 bars. With a win rate of 65.6% and an average outcome of 1.18R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
