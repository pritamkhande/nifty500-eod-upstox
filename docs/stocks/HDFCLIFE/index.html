System Behaviour Commentary

For HDFCLIFE, the system generated 21 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 32.0 bars. With a win rate of 57.1% and an average outcome of 0.52R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
