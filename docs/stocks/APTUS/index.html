System Behaviour Commentary

For APTUS, the system generated 15 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.0 bars. With a win rate of 73.3% and an average outcome of 0.62R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
