System Behaviour Commentary

For GVT&D, the system generated 55 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 37.5 bars. With a win rate of 72.7% and an average outcome of 0.87R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
