System Behaviour Commentary

For TMPV, the system generated 75 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 36.5 bars. With a win rate of 68.0% and an average outcome of 1.15R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
