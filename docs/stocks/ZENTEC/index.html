System Behaviour Commentary

For ZENTEC, the system generated 39 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.6 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 28.7 bars. With a win rate of 51.3% and an average outcome of 0.31R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 2.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -7.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
