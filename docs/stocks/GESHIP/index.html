System Behaviour Commentary

For GESHIP, the system generated 65 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 39.0 bars. With a win rate of 73.8% and an average outcome of 0.94R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
