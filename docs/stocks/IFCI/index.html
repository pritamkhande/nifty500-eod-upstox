System Behaviour Commentary

For IFCI, the system generated 46 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.0 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.3 bars. With a win rate of 47.8% and an average outcome of 0.35R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.4% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.3%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
