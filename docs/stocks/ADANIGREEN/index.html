System Behaviour Commentary

For ADANIGREEN, the system generated 24 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 23.2 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, tilted towards maximising return.
