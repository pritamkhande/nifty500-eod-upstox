System Behaviour Commentary

For KALYANKJIL, the system generated 14 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.9 bars. With a win rate of 78.6% and an average outcome of 0.80R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -1.9%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
