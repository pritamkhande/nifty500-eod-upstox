System Behaviour Commentary

For LTFOODS, the system generated 37 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.9 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.4 bars. With a win rate of 51.4% and an average outcome of 0.43R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.6% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
