System Behaviour Commentary

For REDINGTON, the system generated 57 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.0 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.7 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
