System Behaviour Commentary

For ANGELONE, the system generated 15 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 20.9 bars. With a win rate of 46.7% and an average outcome of 2.37R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -8.6%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
