System Behaviour Commentary

For BBTC, the system generated 68 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.0 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 34.1 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
