System Behaviour Commentary

For INTELLECT, the system generated 35 trades over the full sample, averaging about 3.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 30.9 bars. With a win rate of 68.6% and an average outcome of 0.95R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 6.0% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
