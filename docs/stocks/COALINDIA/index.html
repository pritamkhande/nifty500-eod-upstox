System Behaviour Commentary

For COALINDIA, the system generated 64 trades over the full sample, averaging about 4.2 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 31.8 bars. With a win rate of 65.6% and an average outcome of 0.62R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 5.1% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
