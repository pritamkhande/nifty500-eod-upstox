System Behaviour Commentary

For JPPOWER, the system generated 32 trades over the full sample, averaging about 1.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 29.5 bars. With a win rate of 50.0% and an average outcome of 0.41R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 1.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, designed more for research than raw returns.
