System Behaviour Commentary

For IOB, the system generated 63 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.8 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 35.0 bars. With a win rate of 57.1% and an average outcome of 0.69R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.7% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -3.0%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
