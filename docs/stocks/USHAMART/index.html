System Behaviour Commentary

For USHAMART, the system generated 54 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 33.3 bars. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
