System Behaviour Commentary

For ZFCVINDIA, the system generated 36 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.5 bars. With a win rate of 55.6% and an average outcome of 0.95R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.4%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
