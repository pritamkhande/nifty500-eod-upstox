System Behaviour Commentary

For VGUARD, the system generated 38 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.1 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 49.2 bars. With a win rate of 60.5% and an average outcome of 1.04R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.3% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -2.5%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
