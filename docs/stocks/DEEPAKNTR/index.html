System Behaviour Commentary

For DEEPAKNTR, the system generated 35 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.3 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 38.1 bars. With a win rate of 48.6% and an average outcome of 0.73R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 3.2% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -5.8%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
