System Behaviour Commentary

For PIIND, the system generated 36 trades over the full sample, averaging about 2.5 trades per year. The typical holding period is around 43.2 bars. With a win rate of 77.8% and an average outcome of 0.98R per trade, the equity curve grows at roughly 4.8% CAGR while suffering a maximum drawdown of -4.2%. Overall, this behaves like a very selective, long-term system, with very conservative risk, balanced between robustness and return.
