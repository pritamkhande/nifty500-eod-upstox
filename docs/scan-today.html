How this scan works

The core Gann logic always runs on the full daily bar (H, L, O, C) of the last completed session. That is how swings and squares are detected and how the backtest is generated.

In addition, you are now collecting an early close for every stock – approximately 10 minutes before the market close. If the early close is still within a safe margin from the square levels, then you can pre-enter the trade on the signal date itself, instead of waiting for next-day confirmation.

This page is designed exactly for that: it shows which stocks generated signals, and whether the early-close price keeps the signal safe or already too close to the “flip” point.
